About the Counsel

A Legacy Built on
Trust & Results

Mahendra Gopalani is a distinguished advocate with over two decades of experience practicing before the High Courts and district courts across India. His approach combines rigorous legal scholarship with a deep understanding of his clients' unique circumstances.

Admitted to the Bar Council of India, Mr. Gopalani has successfully represented clients in complex civil, criminal, and corporate matters — earning a reputation for meticulous preparation, eloquent argumentation, and unwavering ethical standards.
