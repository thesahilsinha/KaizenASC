About the Counsel

A Legacy Built on
Trust & Results

Mahinder Kumar is a distinguished advocate based in Delhi, practicing before the Tis Hazari Courts and superior courts across the jurisdiction. Over two decades of legal practice have shaped a counsel known for meticulous preparation, principled advocacy, and deep empathy for every client's circumstances.

With chambers at Ch. No. 328, Civil Wing, Tis Hazari Courts, Mr. Kumar has successfully represented clients across a wide spectrum of civil, criminal, and commercial disputes — earning a reputation that rests firmly on results and integrity.
