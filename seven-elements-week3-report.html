The acceptance rate can be improved by personalising the connection request note — even a single sentence referencing something specific about their company or content increases acceptance by 2–3x. Once content starts publishing in Week 4, profile visits from the connection requests already sent will start to compound — content gives people a reason to accept after the fact.

InMail credits restored — 30 guaranteed inbox hits in Week 4. Unlike connection requests or cold DMs that can be ignored, InMails land directly in the recipient's primary inbox regardless of connection status. With 30 credits available, Week 4 should prioritise these for the highest-value prospects — decision-makers who have already engaged with the email sequence or visited the profile but haven't connected yet.
