05

Bottlenecks Identified

Three structural gaps surfaced in Week 1 that will constrain growth if not resolved before scaling outreach volume.

Pricing conversation arriving too early. Multiple leads showed friction at the ₹45K point — not necessarily because of the number itself, but because the discovery call did not build enough perceived value before the figure was introduced. The fix is structural: price should arrive after a problem statement has been co-created with the prospect, not as a line item in a features list.

Call quality and confidence gap. CRM notes suggest that the pricing section of calls is where the conversation loses momentum. Mock call practice specifically on the pricing segment — including objection handling and reframing the value proposition — is the single highest-leverage improvement available right now.

No cold email or content layer. The pipeline currently runs on one channel. A single-channel pipeline is fragile — it cannot compound. Cold email and video content would add lead sources that run in parallel, increase touchpoints with warm leads, and build brand recall over time.

06

Forward Strategy

The Week 1 learning is clear: outreach works, calls are happening, but the pricing conversation is ending deals before they start. The forward strategy addresses this directly — before adding more volume.

→

Mock call practice — pricing segment

Prioritise structured practice specifically on how pricing is introduced and handled. The goal is confidence, brevity, and timing — not a scripted pitch. Run at least 3 mock calls this week before the next live call.

→

Continue LinkedIn outreach — maintain volume

Outreach is the strongest active channel. Keep 20–30 messages per week going. Refine targeting criteria to better filter for decision-makers with active brand needs. Outreach quality over quantity.

→

Cold email — activate with verification first

Build a prospect list with verified emails before sending a single message. Unverified lists damage sender reputation. Tool recommendation: Apollo or Hunter for prospecting and verification. Prioritise decision-makers in target industries.

↗

LinkedIn + Instagram video content

The highest-leverage long-term channel. Short-form video showing brand thinking, process, and perspective builds trust with prospects before they ever speak to you. Start with 1 video per week — consistency over perfection.

↗

Follow up the warm lead within 48 hours

The collaborative-energy lead from this week is the most time-sensitive action in the entire pipeline right now. A clear, brief follow-up — proposing next steps, not asking if they're interested — keeps momentum going.
