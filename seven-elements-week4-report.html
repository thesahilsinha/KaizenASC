02

Email Campaign Performance

After the volume surge in Week 3, email activity retreated in Week 4 — both in volume and quality. 79 emails against a target of 100+ represents a meaningful shortfall, and the open rate regression to 35% suggests either list fatigue on the current segment or a subject line that underperformed relative to the Week 3 copy.

Week 3 produced 50 opened emails from 110 sent. Week 4 produced only ~28 from 79 — a significant contraction in absolute reach. The pipeline of contacts in deep follow-up (built from Weeks 1–3) remains the most valuable asset right now and must be actively worked in Week 5 with personalised close attempts.

03

Ad Performance — Video & Second Ad

The video ad went live as planned and reached over 2,000 people — a meaningful reach milestone for the campaign. However, impressions without any calls generated signals a disconnect between the creative or targeting and the intended action. Reach is not impact. The second ad remains in process, stalled on audience filter refinement.

2,000+ impressions with zero calls is a targeting or creative signal problem — the ad is reaching people, but not the right people, or the CTA is not compelling enough to act. Before the second ad goes live, audience filters must be tightened: geography, industry, company size, and decision-maker job title should all be explicitly defined. The video ad should also be reviewed — the hook (first 3 seconds) and the CTA (end card or caption) are the two most likely failure points. Running more spend without fixing these will multiply the same zero-conversion result.

07

Week 5 Recovery Plan

Week 5 is a recovery and escalation week. Every delayed item from Week 4 executes on day one. New channels — cold calls, Instagram outreach, and LinkedIn ads — activate in parallel. The mail tool is live, which means newsletter deployment has no remaining technical blocker. This is the week the campaign either regains momentum or confirms a structural problem.

📞
Cold Calls — Warm Prospects First
Prioritise contacts who have opened emails 2+ times. A call backed by prior email context converts at 3–4x the rate of a fully cold call. Keep calls brief, specific, and outcome-focused — one clear ask per call.
New channel
📱
Instagram Outreach — List Ready, Deploy Now
The Instagram outreach list is compiled and ready. This channel activates in Week 5 — DMs targeting founders and brand managers whose businesses show visual identity gaps. Every message should reference something specific from their feed. The list is ready; execution is the only variable.
List ready · new channel
💼
LinkedIn Ads — Launch
LinkedIn ads go live in Week 5 with tightly defined audience filters: industry, company size, job title, and geography locked before spend begins. Do not replicate the video ad's targeting — use the 2K-impression data to narrow the audience before scaling.
Paid activation
📰
Newsletter — Deploy Day One
The mail tool is live. The newsletter has no remaining blocker. It deploys in the first 24 hours of Week 5 — not mid-week, not "when ready." This channel has been delayed two weeks and the list has been waiting unused.
Overdue — priority
